Who We Are?

Welcome to Los Angeles Pet Adoption Service, where our love for animals drives everything we do. As a team of dedicated animal welfare advocates, we are committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming pets in need throughout the Los Angeles area. Our mission is simple: to connect every homeless pet with a loving family. We believe in making responsible matches between pets and adopters, creating lifelong bonds that enrich lives. Our organization not only focuses on adoption but also educates the community on responsible pet ownership and the importance of spaying and neutering. Join us in our heartfelt mission to bring joy and companionship to both pets and people, one adoption at a time.
